# stampcheckertool
A Sea of Thieves Stamp Tracker/Checker

_This application simply monitors the internet traffic between you and `Sea Of Thieves.exe` (the process);
the application determines which traffic is relevant (your stamp); and then it presents it to you, allowing you
to share it with others._ 

**This does NOT violate Sea Of Thieves Terms Of Service**

# Features
- A tracker that finds what Sea of Thieves server you are connected to (it will update if your stamp changes)
- Add/Remove Friends
- Set your visibility: `Public, Friends, Private`. This controls who can see your latest stamp
- Whitelist: A list of users that can always see your latest stamp even if your `Visibility=Private` 
- Blacklist: A list of users that are completely forbidden from seeing your latest stamp even if your `Visibility=Public`
- Search users: If a user has whitelisted you, you will be able to see their latest stamp even if you are not there friend. Otherwise, latest stamp is only attached to users in your Friends List.

# How to install? 
1. Download `Stamps_Setup.exe` (the installer). It is at the bottom of this page located in `Assets`.
2. Run `Stamps_Setup.exe`
3. Follows the steps
4. In the installer, you can choose to launch the application. Otherwise, you can look for the `Stamps App` shortcut on your `Desktop` or search it in your `Windows Search Bar`
5. Done!

# How to use the application?
1. Start `Sea of Thieves`
2. Run `Stamps App` (On Desktop or search `Stamps App` in Windows Search Bar)
3. You will login with Discord OAuth
7. Once logged in, there is a toggle tracking button that you will turn on to start tracking
8. If you close the application and do not logout, you will auto-login next time

**Note: You may see non-sense stamps if you launch Stamps App in the Sea of Thieves login screen, Main Menu, or somewhere Not-in-Game. This is fine, but it may be inaccurate at this stage. It will properly update once you load a boat and enter the server; allow the tracker to adjust itself; it could take a moment.** 

# How to uninstall?
1. Search `Add or Remove Programs` in the Windows Search Bar 
2. Search `Stamps App` and double click, click uninstall, and follow the steps

A secondary way is to right click on the `Stamps App` on Desktop or Windows Search Bar and look for the uninstall option
